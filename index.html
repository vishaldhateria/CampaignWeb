The campaign ‘If I Am Born’ consists of a series of stories and features of real people who want to make their voice heard. This Campaign also strives to bring to life issues that everyday people face, whether it is gender inequality or environmental problems, the campaign transcends any particular issue. All you have to do is choose a topic from the list below and write about it or send us a video or a photo essay. We will then broadcast your story to the world.

Here’s some thought starters:

People usually read stories and blogs because they are personal. Your stories could be an incident, something you’ve experienced in life or even something you think needs to change. Sometimes writing a story from a different perspective, or shooting a short film based on your idea of the issue, putting yourself in the shoes of someone else gives birth to good art.

  • You can write about how poverty causes famers to commit suicide.
  • You could also write a letter from the perspective of a poor man.
  • You could compare India to other countries when it comes to poverty.
  • Another way could be writing a short story or poem on this topic.
  • List down a bunch of must see rags to riches movies.
  • A story on a personality who strives to uphold the rights of the poor.

Headline- If I Am Born… Polluted

  • You can write about how pollution is affecting the environment around you.
  • You can write about how we should take a stand against pollution.
  • A video on ways to curb pollution.
  • A poem or short film on pollution.
  • Tell a story from the perspective of a tree.

Now some ground rules for writing:

  • The stories should be related to the topic you’ve selected and also in correlation to the theme of the campaign.
  • Do not exceed the limit of 500 words. Keep it short and hard-hitting.
